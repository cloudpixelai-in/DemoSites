FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
Do you accept health insurance? +
Yes, we accept 20+ major insurance plans including Star Health, HDFC Ergo, New India Assurance, United India and ICICI Lombard. Please carry your insurance card during your visit.
How do I book an appointment? +
You can book an appointment through our website form above, call us directly, or walk in to our clinic. Online bookings receive SMS confirmation within 30 minutes.
Are home visits available? +
Yes, we offer home visits for elderly and bedridden patients within a 10 km radius. Home collection for lab tests is also available every day from 7 AM to 10 AM.
What are your clinic timings? +
Morning OPD: 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM. Evening OPD: 4:00 PM – 8:00 PM. Monday to Saturday. Emergency services are available 24×7. Closed on Sundays (Emergency only).
Do you have a diagnostic lab? +
Yes, our in-house diagnostic lab offers blood tests, urine analysis, X-ray, ECG and ultrasound. Reports for most tests are available on the same day. Online report access coming soon.
Can I consult online (teleconsultation)? +
Yes, we offer video consultations for follow-up visits and non-emergency cases. Book through our website and receive a video call link. Teleconsultation is available Mon–Sat, 10 AM – 6 PM.
